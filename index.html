Brewarrina Unity 3D

An immersive Unity game that delivers an interactive storytelling experience. The main objective was to craft an engaging interface that enables users to connect uniquely with the narrative. The story revolves around the Brewarrina Aboriginal Fish Traps, culturally significant heritage-listed structures located on the Barwon River in Brewarrina, New South Wales, Australia. These traps are also referred to as Baiame's Ngunnhu, Nonah, or Nyemba Fish Traps.
